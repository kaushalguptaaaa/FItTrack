About FitTrack

KG

Kaushal Gupta

Developer

I built FitTrack as a web programming course project. My goal was to make a clean, lightweight workout tracker that saves everything locally in the browser and respects your privacy.

Why FitTrack?

FitTrack is a simple workout tracker made for a college summer project. It helps you log your exercises and view your weekly progress. Everything runs completely offline in your browser, meaning no signup, no databases, and no internet connection is required.

How it Works

A simple breakdown of how your workout data is processed and shown offline:

1. Add Workout
You type in your sets, reps, and duration on the dashboard.
2. Saved Locally
Your browser saves the data directly to Local Storage.
3. Instant Update
Your dashboard stats, weekly chart, and calendar update immediately.
